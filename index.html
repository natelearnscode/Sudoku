Sudoku:

Sudoku is a classic puzzle game in which you try to fill a 9 by 9 grid with the digits 1-9. The goal is for each column, row, and 3 by 3 box to be filled with all the digits from 1 to 9. Digits may not be repeated in a column, row, or box. For more info, go to Sudoku's Wikipedia page.

Controls

Click on the grid and either use your keyboard or the buttons below to input digits.
